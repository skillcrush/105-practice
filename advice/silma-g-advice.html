Hi, my name is Silma and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is your friend!

Being confused is normal. Being confused means you will learn something new.

Confusion, therefore, is a sign that you are heading in the right direction.

Continue to challenge yourself

Sometimes it seems hard and maybe you will take a while to learn something or move forward. That's okay. You WILL get it and once you do it'll be easy.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

Always learn new things. Never stop pushing yourself to understand the things that don't make sense right now.
