Hi, my name is Ken and I have some advice for you!

YOU CAN DO THIS!!!

Everyone starts at where you are now! You have to keep a positive additude. Just keep at it. Nothing worthwile is ever easy. Learning how to code is a process. It take effort and perseverance. You got this!!! Bring your "A" game!!!

When you make a mistake, that's great! Go back and figure out what you did wrong. Ask questions, ask for help. Everytime you make a mistake, you are learning. The more you code the easier it becomes.

Bring your passion with you!!!

There will be times when your so confused you won't know which way is up. Don't beat your head on the computer screen trying to figure it out. Go take a break. Get some coffee, take a walk, clear your head. When you come back you will be amazed how easy it is to see the issue.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You see when you encounter a new programming language part of your learning process includes learning to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand what it's doing.
