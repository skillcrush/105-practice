“But the most important thing you can do is to NOT GIVE UP, it’s challenging even for computer naturals, you may think you are the most stupid person in the universe, several times, and it will seam impossible, but it’s not!!! Yep, It’s good for a developer to have good logic, math and problem solving skills, but people are not born with those, even if they have a certain easiness with it, they still have to learn, they still have to practice, and if they can do it, YOU CAN TOO, all you need is patience, discipline and a lot of will.”

“When you get sick of theory, go for practice, when you start to go crazy because something just isn’t working, go back to theory...”
