Hello, my name is Kristi. I have some advice for you!

Don't be afraid to make mistakes.

Learning something new is challenging but can also be rewarding. You will not master everything right away, and that's a good thing. Mistakes are often our best teachers and will reinforce important concepts, so don't fear them!

Frustration is normal; take a break to avoid feeling overwhelmed.

When you are learning a new technology, the amount of new information coming at you can be overwhelming. Getting stuck on any step in a new process can cause a lot of frustration. It's ok to take a break when this feeling sets in and return to a problem later with a fresh set of eyes.

Believe in yourself, no matter what.

Confidence in taking a new path may not be there every day, but keep moving forward. Difficult coding concepts will get easier with practice and time.
You are worth the effort, and it will pay off - keep it up!
