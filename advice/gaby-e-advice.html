Hi, my name is Gaby and I have some advice for you!

Don't give up!

When you start learning how to code, you will question if you did the right decision. You won't understand the terms or the assigments you are doing.

This feeling is normal. Relax and breathe but never quit! After you push through the whole course, suddendently a light bulb will turn on.

Trust the process.

It goes hand in hand with what I mentioned in the paragraph above. Do everything on your part that is required. Do your lessons every week.

If you fill stuck, reach out to the Skillcrush community. You have many resources. Be sure to use them.
