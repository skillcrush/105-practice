Hi, my name is Carolyn and I have some advice for you!

Don't be afraid to make mistakes!

Programs and web pages are never perfect the first time around, no matter how good you think your logic is. Making mistakes is normal, and how you learn.

Play around with different ideas.

Try out new things. Try variations of something you're already doing.

Don't be afraid to look for help or ask for help.

There are a number of great resources on the web, and there are also people around you who've been where you are and will be able to help.

Test early and often.

Test after any major change, and also after merging with other people's changes to make sure that yoru changes and their changes don't interact badly.

and above all...

Have fun!!
