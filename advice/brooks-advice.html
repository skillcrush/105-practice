Just Keep Swimming!

There are going to be so many times where you feel like you are drowning in the sea of knowledge that is coding. Keep treading the water and "Just keep swimming", as Dory would say. When you feel like you are stuck or drowning, there are a few key things to do so as not to have a meltdown and throw your computer out the window.

  1. Take a break and breathe ("Goos-fraba, Goos-fraba")
  2. Google your issues
  3. Talk to other coders about any problems
  4. Try to come back at the problem with fresh eyes
  5. Be POSITIVE, practice makes perfect.
