Hi, my name is Christina, I'm a historian turned techie and I want to share some advice with you!

Take the leap, it will be worth it!

My very best advice to you as a newbie is to stop googling three more weeks "in case you haven't seen ALL the options yet", make a decision and LETs GO! You will never feel ready but every day you wait is a day you could have been making progress towards reaching your goals.

Yes, YOU can do it, too!

I just finished my Master's in History so not a tech related field AT ALL. In school I was terrible at maths, or any sciences for that matter. Yet here I am, I already finished the HTML and CSS course, and I can follow all the lessons. Even though I'm not good at maths or have a scientist's brain. The truth is: You have no idea of all the things you can do until you do them.
