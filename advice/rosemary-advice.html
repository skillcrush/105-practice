Hey, I'm Rosemary and I'm here to share some tips as you learn to code!

Failure is your friend!

As you learn to code, you will hit pitfalls and rough patches that come with learning any new skill. Keep going! As long as you are moving forward, you will break through the obstacles one by one until you are an expert.

Failure is part of the learning process! Learn from the mistakes and keep forging ahead. Though it may be cliche, it rings true that you learn more from the failures than you do the successes. Let the failures drive you forward to success!

Take advantage of online resources.

The internet is full of helpful tips and tricks for someone learning to code! If you find yourself stuck, a quick online search may be all you need to find a solution.

Coding schools like Skillcrush can be really effective ways to learn more about coding, especially if you are looking to become an expert! Structured classes with a community of professional developers goes a long way in catapulting your coding career.

Keep your chin up and your eye on the prize!

When you are learning any new skill, it can be so easy to beat yourself up at every mistake or get frustrated when the process takes longer than you expected. When you get discouraged remember that you are learning something brand new and this takes time! You will get the hang of it, just as you have with other new skills.

Be proud of yourself for taking the leap to dive into something new! Keep looking ahead at the successes your new skills will bring!
