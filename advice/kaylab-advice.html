Hi my name is Kayla and I have some advice for you!

There's Always Help

Programming isn't a skill that people are born with. Sometimes it might feel like others are just more gifted, or a lot of times, better at math. There is a place for you in this field if this is what you want to do. There is always help if you need it.

Be Kind to Yourself

I am one of those people that hates when I'm not immediately good at something. Is it realistic? Absolutely not. Do I still get upset at first? Yes. This process has taught me that failure is required to get better and beating yourself up only makes the process harder. Remember to be kind to yourself while you learn.
