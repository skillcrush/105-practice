Hi, my name is Dan and I have some advice for you!

Utilize Resources!

When going through course material, make sure to take the time to save and organize all linked material to a respective Bookmarks folder in your browser.

Make sure to explore these materials in tandem with the course subject so you have a greater depth of understanding.

Check out the student community on the Skillcrush Slack channel for guidance and questions you may have.

Be Patient!

Sometimes you may feel stuck or lost on a subject. Make sure if you encounter something that frustrates you that you take some time to step away from the work for a bit to recharge and refocus.

Don't feel bad if you need to go back and review some of the topics you've already gone through in order to get a better grasp.
