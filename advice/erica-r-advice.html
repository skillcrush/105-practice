Hi, my name is Erica, and I have some advice for you!

Be Persistent

You probably won't do everything right the first time. Just keep practicing and eventually it will click.

Ask Questions

Sometimes you will get really stuck. If that happens, ask an instrutor or classmate for help. Don't forget...Google is your friend!

Take Breaks

Don't let yourself get burned out. Take a break and watch The Office every now and then.
