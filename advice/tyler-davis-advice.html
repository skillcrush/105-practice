Hi, my name is Tyler and I have some advice for you!

Commit, Commit, Commit.

You can't learn a whole field by giving it one day a week. You can't be a great entrepreneur working 1 hour a day. Life is about creating purpose. Try your best, and your best will be given back to you.

i've quit my job at a costco after 5 years, committed to learning software development in as many forms as i can. I worked in a corporate buying office, an accounting office, on a forklift, and multiple warehouse positions. It's a rough rough life in corporate retail so i have decided to taket that effort which i was putting into being great there, now, into myself to create something better for myself.

Keep your head up

It's all just learning, and working. There is always a task to be done, and it is up to you to understand the priority in which said tasks hold.

Once you can maintain an understanding and execution of that priority the rest is clockwork.

Do everything you can to understand what is in front of you, and if you don't get it, don't get frusterated. Either do proper research on the web, or just take a few minutes to walk away and have a breather.
