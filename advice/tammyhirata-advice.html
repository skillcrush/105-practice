Hi, my name is Tammy, and this is some advice...

Keep your eyes on the prize!

When you get discouraged, distracted, or frustrated, remember why you are doing this, and let that motivate you.

You're not an imposter.

If you are doubting your abilities and feeling like you don't belong, switch it up on that negative self-doubt, and doubt that doubt!

Ask for help.

There is no shame in asking for help, and help is sometimes the most efficient way to get something done.
