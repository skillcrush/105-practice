Hi, My Name is Karyn and I Have Some Advice for You!

Rome Wasn't Built in a Day

Fight the urge to master all of your new skills overnight. These things take time.

Practice your skills consistently and you'll see results sooner.

Don't Forget to Have Fun

While you're still learning, remind yourself to that this is all supposed to be fun.

Today's mistake is tomorrow's funny story. Try to laugh as much as possible.
