Hi, my name is Tony

and I have some advice for you!

Be true to yourself.

The only person you are ever guaranteed to have with you for the rest of your life is yourself; therefore, make sure to live in such a way that honors your own heart!

You are embarking on this adventure into the wonderful world of programming because you are choosing to do what is right for yourself, even though others in your life might not understand or appreciate it. That's okay; know that even though you might feel uncertain, you are on the right path.

Stay in action.

Learning to program for 5 minutes a day for a year will get you further than thinking about learning to program for an hour every day, but never doing it. But actually no.

Action is what takes hopes and dreams and turns them into reality.

Don't "should" on your self. The tortoise beats the hare every time. Small, consistent wins every day are better than huge wins for a few days followed by burnout.
