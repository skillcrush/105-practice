Hi, my name is Luke and I have some advice for you!

Build structural html with color borders

Put different colored borders around your 'Sections', as well as'Full-Width & Half-Width Div element'. This will help you visualize the layout of your web page even with minimal content.

Supplement your courses with books

Though Skillcrush is amazing, web development is long-term investment and what you get out of your work is based on what you put in; therfore, reading is an important addition to your studies. Websites like Sitepoint.com provide excellent libraries on all things front-end development and more!

Build additional projects

Go find websites on the internet that you could give an excellent overhaul to. A great start is a local non-profit who does not require much more than a static websit for info. Every new lesson or concept you learn, apply to your project and eventually offer to donate the site.
