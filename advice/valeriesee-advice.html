Hi, my name is Valerie and I have some advice for you!

Practice. Practice. Practice.

The absolute most important thing I could reccommend to be successful in this course is to immediately apply whatever is covered to a practical project. I know that there are plenty of opportunities to do so within the course, but to ensure you remember the process it helps a ton to practice on your own.

Read and Research

There are external resources provided in the course,but there is a whole internet out there full of resources waiting for you to dive into! Learning the same material from different perspectives provides a greater understanding and may offer a learning style that is better suited to your needs.

Be Persistant.

Unfortunately not every bit of code you write is going to be perfect on the first go. Be patient with yourself and do not give up. If you need to step away from a frustrating portion of the class, go put on some tea, clear your head and get right back at it.
