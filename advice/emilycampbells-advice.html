Hi my name is Emily Campbell and I have some advice for you!

Get out of your house!

Taking an online class can feel isolating at times. I try to go to a coffee shop when I can to be in a different environment and not get distracted by chores I have to get done at home.

Failure leads to learning!

The more you fail, the more you can learn from your mistakes. Sometimes the concepts I know the best, took me the longest to figure out. Once you do though, it will stick in your brain forever!
