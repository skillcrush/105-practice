Your willpower has limits!

Like any of your capacities, your willpower has limits. Don't think that you can spend 20 hours a day working on your goals. Your first priority should be to not give up. If you can make sure that you actually are working on it, and that you aren't neglecting yourself, then work on doing more.

Don't give up!

In order to make sure that you are actually working enough on it, give yourself goals that you can actually accomplish, and reward yourself for completing them. You need to be honest with yourself and not make excuses to yourself. Learn to spot and dispute your own bull$#*%!

Be nice to yourself!

This one is probably a bit more specific to me. Keep a checklist of things you need to do for your own morale, and do those things. Have you eaten today? How much sleep did you get? Shower? Meds? Bills? Homework? Cleaned? Have you found time to relax for a minute? Have you said something nice about yourself today? Have you worked on your mental health today? Try to remember that this isn't going to change overnight and you need to work on it like everything else.

Make things easy!

The point of code and mathematics is to make your life easier. Don't just code for other people's benefit. Code tools to make your own life easier. Customize some boilerplate for your projects, make macros for your text editor, make assets for yourself. Work for you too.
