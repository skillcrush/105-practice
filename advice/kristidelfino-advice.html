Hi my name is Kristi and I have some advice for you!

Don't get overwhelmed

You're learning a lot, so try not to overwhelm yourself by taking in too much at a time. Brain breaks are good!

If you feel like you're losing touch, walk away for a day or two. Practice your favorite hobby, spend time with family, etc. You'll come back more refreshed and ready to focus.

Believe in yourself

It sounds silly and cliché but believe that you can do this. This is, for me, the hardest thing! I constantly need to remind myself that I'm smart and capable enough to learn this stuff and make my goals a reality. Look at yourself in the mirror and give yourself a pep talk. Make a vision board. Keep a list of your other, non-tech accomplishments as a reminder of what you can achieve when you work at something. Anything to remind yourself that you are awesome.

Find a community

Whether it's the Skillcrush Slack group, a Facebook group, a Meetup, or just a group of supportive friends, find a community to support you. You will have questions, and you will have doubts. Having people there to bounce ideas around and give you a little boost is crucial to success.
