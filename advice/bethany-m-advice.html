Always be ready to learn

Technology is changing at a rapid pace and you need to be able to learn and adapt. There's no way one person can know it all. Be ready to learn and grow constantly. Bookmark resources, learn how to word your search queries to get helpful results, and build a community that can help you when you get stuck.

Find other developers & designers that you want to emulate

See what they're saying and doing. Follow in their footsteps. Follow them on Twitter, watch their videos on YouTube, appreciate their work on Behance. Take it one step further and engage with them - comment on their posts, ask questions, sign up for their email list if they have one.

Remember it's okay to take a break

Sometimes coding and designing can be frustrating. You might need to step away for a bit to clear your head or think about something else. You'd be surprised how many times the solution will come to you in the shower or after a night's rest. Don't be too hard on yourself.
