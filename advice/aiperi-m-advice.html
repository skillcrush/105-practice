Hi, my name is Aiperi and I have some advice for you!

Be patient with yourself!

When you start learning a new programming language, it's going to be hard and it gets more confusing & challenging along the way. I haven't yet met anyone for whom learning coding was easy, so be patient and keep moving forward. Do not ever give up and always ask for help if you need one!

Support from Instructors & peers is very important!

Honestly, I don't know if I would keep moving forward and studying if I didn't have all the support from my Skillcrush inctructors. They are so helpful & supportive whenever I'm stuck or confused. Also, my husband is always there for me and inspires me to believe in myself and that I can do it! Really grateful for them!
