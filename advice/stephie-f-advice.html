Hi, my name is Stephie and I have some advice for you!

It's pretty simple stuff...

Just start.
Don't be afraid to make mistakes.
Commit to at least one hour a day. Put it in your schedule if that helps!
If a whole hour seems like a lot, take a 5-10 minute break after 30 minutes.
If it ever seems too hard, remember that even your instructors had to learn all of this too.
Reach out to other students when you need help or want support.
Keep going.
