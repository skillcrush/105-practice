Can't Keep Away From Coding?

- advice for a long lasting relationship with code.

Learn the basics

Code that looks complicated are nothing but many pieces of basic code put together.

By learning the basics you will master the tools!

Code every day

Practice your coding skills by applying your knowledge on your own projects.

Keep on coding on a regular basis!

Get a life

Stay fit, meet with friends, get enough sleep. Your mental state affects your productivity.

Giving yourself a break is a cheap investment in your success!
