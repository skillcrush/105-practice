Advice for Coding Newbs

-- Erin P's Advice

Spend time on the exercises. Don't try to fly through everything to get on to the next thing. Take the time to learn from doing, from making things work that you aren't sure you know how to do. You'll learn much more from figuring things out than from following pre-set exercises.

This is advice to myself as much as to anyone else.
